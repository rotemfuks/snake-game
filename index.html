The Snake Game

How To play the Snake game:
1. Control the snake's movement using arrow keys.
2. Guide the snake to eat food and make it grow longer.
3. Avoid collisions with walls or the snake's own body.
4. The game ends if the snake hits an obstacle.
5. Aim for a high score by eating as much food as possible without crashing. Have fun!
6. To start agian click the refresh button on your browser. Have fun!
